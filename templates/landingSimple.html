About GuaRENTeed


After having successive bad experiences with rental properties across both Bristol and London we decided to set up GuaRENTeed to address imbalance of power between tenants and landlords.

We have one aim, to provide tenants with the information they need to make informed decisions about rental properties. Too often, tenants are forced to make snap decisions about properties as landlords and estate agents who, in a 5 minute house viewing, can - literally and metaphorically - paper over the cracks.

After having successive bad experiences with rental properties across both Bristol and London we decided to set up GuaRENTeed to shift this balance of power.

Please note this service is in its pilot stage so please be patient with any technical or design issues. All our reviews are based on information provided to us by former tenants. We are not responsible for the accuracy, content or completeness, reliability of any information provided to us. If you have any issue with anything that has been written then please get in touch with us our email.
