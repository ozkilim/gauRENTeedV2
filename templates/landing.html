About GuaRENTeed


Our aim is to address the imbalance of power between landlords and tenants.

Too often, tenants in the private rental market are forced to make snap decisions based on incomplete information, fundamentally favouring landlords and estate agents who, in a 5 minute house viewing, can - literally and metaphorically - paper over the cracks. .

After having successive bad experiences with rental properties across both Bristol and London we decided to set up GuaRENTeed to a shift this balance of power.

We want to give tenants more information about the place they are looking to rent so they can make a more informed decision about the property and ultimately have a better rental experience.

Please note that the service is in its pilot stage. We would ask that you be patient with any technical issues, and do let us know if/ when these appear using the form at the bottom of the page.
