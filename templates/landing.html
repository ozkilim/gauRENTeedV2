About Guarenteed


Our aim is to address the inbalence of power between landlords and tennents. After having successive bad experiences with rental properties in the bristol and London we set up guarnteed to address the inbalence of power between landlords and tennents. We want to give tennants more infromation about the place they are looking to rent so they can make a more informed decision about the property and ultimately have a better rental experience. We are running a pilot of the idea in brisol. This is very much a pilot to please be patuent and understanding about any issues you see.
